origins of $DOGE

Many of you might not know that the origins of $DOGE trace back to the TV show Homestar Runner.

The use of the misspelled word "DOG" actually goes back to June 24, 2005, when it appeared in a Homestar Runner episode.

In it, $RUNNER calls Strong Bad his "D-O-G-E" in an attempt to distract him from his work.

And that's how $DOGE was created.
